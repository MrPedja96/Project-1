About


History

Frozen Moonlight is a melodic death metal/metalcore band from Novi Sad, Serbia formed by vocalist/bassist Mladen Nikolić and guitarist Branko Andrej Živković in 2013. Their first line up also included Predrag Andrejević on guitar and Boris Ždero on drums. In 2014 they released their debut EP Dat Victoria Vires as well as introducing a new bassist to the band, Igor Korom, which allowed Mladen to hone his vocal and compositional skills. That year they also win the best Serbian metal release of the year award from Global Metal Apocalypse, which put them on the list with the likes of Deathstars, Motionless in White, Behemoth etc. In 2015 Predrag, Boris and Igor left the band due to personal and academic reasons. Nikola Juhas became their new bassist, Vanja Andrić (Hollow My Eyes, Aheront, Reforma) filling in on either guitar or bass (as well as back or second vocals, lyrics writer and composer) and their new drummer was Antonio Maletin (Rain Delay). The band released two singles during 2016 and 2017: “Kintsukuroi” and “Cinder”. Predrag would soon after rejoin the band replacing Vanja on guitars. During 2018 the band focused on live performances, playing a multitude of venues in and outside of Vojvodina. The bands line-up changes yet again, Antonio leaving because of health complications and Nikola Juhas was kicked out as the result of various conflicts, after being part of Frozen Moonlight for 2 years as a live member. In his place comes Rodoljub Raičković (producer from Vortex Studios in Inđija) and Boris Ždero returns as more of a session member. At the start of 2019 the band introduces a third guitarist, Igor Bubulj and Vanja focuses more on the visual identity of the band as well as writing lyrics. They announce their new EP “Afterglow” to be published in 2019.
